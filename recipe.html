Spicy Fried Chicken



My family loves chicken and today I will be sharing with you one of favourite recipes. Spicy fried chicken, who doesn't like to spice it up a little bit every now and then. This is one recipe your family will enjoy cooking together for years to come.

Ingredients

For the chicken

  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 family pack of chicken

For the batter

  1. 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1 tablespoon black pepper
  3. 1 tablespoon cayenne pepper
  4. 1 teaspoon salt
  5. 1 teaspoon garlic powder

Instructions

Combine ingredients for buttermilk mixture in a large bowl. Put the chicken in the bowl making sure all pieces are covered completely. Marinate chicken for at least 8 hours in the refrigerator. Remove chicken from the refrigerator. In a large paper or plastic bag mix all ingredients for batter. A couple of pieces at a time, add chicken to the seasoned flour and shake off any excess and lay them on a platter at room temp for 20 minutes.

Heat 1/2 inch oil in a pan that can withstand the heat. Heat until a pinch of flour sizzles in the pan. Pan fry until golden brown all over, about 8 to 10 minutes per side, flipping the chicken as needed. Remove to a rack using tongs to drain excess oil.
